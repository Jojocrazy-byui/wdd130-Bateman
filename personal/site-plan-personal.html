Content

Home page

I started ABC's & 123'S Preschool in October 2001. The preschool is in my home in Etna, Wyoming. I use my Bachelor's Degree in Elementary Education and a love of children and reading to help prepare children for kindergarten. As I supplement the teaching the children get at home, I love watching the children learn and grow.

Calender

Calender for 20245-25

The calendar has our Show and Tell dates. We have Show and Tell once a month. The calendar also lets you know any days that we don't have preschool for holidays or breaks. The best part of the calendar- at least for the kids- are special days and parties. Email reminders will also be sent as these dates approach.
